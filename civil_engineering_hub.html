About Civil Engineering

What is Civil Engineering?

Civil engineering is arguably the oldest engineering discipline. It deals with the built environment and can be dated to the first time someone placed a roof over his or her head or laid a tree trunk across a river. It encompasses the planning, design, construction, maintenance, and operation of infrastructure.

Future Scope & Tech

Modern civil engineering integrates advanced technologies like Building Information Modeling (BIM), IoT sensors for structural health monitoring, Drone Surveying, 3D Concrete Printing, and Smart City Planning. The demand for sustainable and earthquake-resistant structures is higher than ever.

Core Domains

Building Const.

Design and erection of residential & commercial buildings.

Bridge Engg.

Analysis and design of bridges to cross physical obstacles.

Transportation

Highway design, traffic management, and pavement.

Railway Engg.

Design, construction and operation of railway systems.

Airport Engg.

Runway design, terminal planning, and air traffic infra.

Water Resources

Dams, canals, irrigation, and fluid mechanics.

Environmental

Water treatment, waste management, and pollution control.

Geotechnical

Soil mechanics, foundation design, and earthworks.
