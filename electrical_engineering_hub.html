What is Electrical Engineering?

परिचय (Introduction)

Electrical Engineering इंजीनियरिंग की वह शाखा है जो Electricity, Electromagnetism, और Electronics के अध्ययन और अनुप्रयोग (application) से संबंधित है। यह आधुनिक दुनिया की रीढ़ है।

महत्व (Importance)

हमारे घरों की बिजली से लेकर विशाल Power Plants, Electric Vehicles (EV), और Automation तक, हर जगह Electrical Engineers की आवश्यकता है।

Why Choose EE?

Government Jobs (JE), PSU (NTPC, PowerGrid), Private Sector Automation, और Entrepreneurship में असीमित अवसर उपलब्ध हैं।

Complete SBTE Roadmap

Semester-wise Journey from 1st to 6th Semester

Semester 1

  • Basic Engineering Mathematics
  • Applied Physics
  • Basic Electrical Engineering
  • Engineering Drawing
  • Fundamental of IT

Semester 2

  • Applied Mathematics
  • Applied Chemistry
  • Communication Skills
  • Fundamentals of Electronic Engg.
  • Engineering Mechanics

Semester 3 Core Start

  • Electrical Circuit and Network
  • Electrical Measurements
  • DC Machines and Transformers

Semester 4

  • AC Machines
  • Power Electronics
  • Microprocessor
  • Control System and PLC

Semester 5 Advanced

  • Switchgear and Protection
  • Solar and Wind Energy
  • Energy Conservation

Semester 6 Final Step

  • Utilization of Electrical Energy
  • Electrical Installation
  • Industrial Drives
  • Project Work

Subject Explorer

Sem 3

Circuit & Network

Kirchhoff's laws, Theorems (Thevenin, Norton), AC fundamentals. यह पूरे Electrical Engineering का आधार है।

Weightage: High | Career: Core Design
Sem 3 & 4

Electrical Machines

Transformers, DC Motors, Induction Motors और Alternators की working और construction.

Weightage: Very High | Career: PSU, Core Industry
Sem 4

Power Electronics

Thyristors, Inverters, Choppers. Electric Vehicles और modern control systems में इसका सबसे ज्यादा उपयोग है।

Weightage: Medium | Career: EV, Automation
Sem 5

Switchgear & Protection

Relays, Circuit Breakers, और power system protection. Grid failures से बचने के लिए महत्वपूर्ण।

Weightage: High | Career: Power Grid, State Board

Career Opportunities

Government Jobs (JE)

  • SSC JE: CPWD, MES etc.
  • RRB JE: Indian Railways
  • State Boards: BSPHCL (Bihar)
  • PSUs: PGCIL, NTPC, NHPC, DVC

Starting Salary: ₹35,000 - ₹50,000 / month

Private Sector & Tech

  • Core Companies: Tata Power, L&T, Adani
  • Automation: Siemens, ABB, Schneider
  • Renewable: Solar plants, Wind energy
  • EV Industry: Battery design, Motor control

Starting Salary: ₹15,000 - ₹30,000 / month
