Frequently Asked Questions

POLYTECHNIC HUB - FAQ

What is POLYTECHNIC HUB?
POLYTECHNIC HUB is an educational platform that provides notes, PDFs, previous year questions, MCQs, test series, video lectures, and important updates for Polytechnic students.
Is registration free?
Yes. Registration is free. Some premium courses or resources may require payment.
How can I download notes and PDFs?
After logging into your account, open the required subject and click the Download or View PDF button.
Can I watch video lectures?
Yes. Video lectures are available for selected subjects and semesters.
How do I reset my password?
Click on the Forgot Password option on the Login page and follow the instructions.
Which branches are supported?
Electrical, Civil, Mechanical, Computer Science, Electronics, and other Polytechnic branches are supported.
Can I use this website on mobile?
Yes. POLYTECHNIC HUB is fully mobile-friendly and works on Android, iPhone, tablets, and desktops.
How can I contact support?
You can contact us through the Contact page or the support email available on the website.
